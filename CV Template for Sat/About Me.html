About Me



My Story

I went to the School of Science and Technology, Singapore after PSLE. In secondary 3, we were offered the choice of an "extra" subject. Among the choices, I picked Media Studies. I was taught how to handle basic camera and audio equipment. More than that however, I found my passion in Production. From there, I aimed to go into a Polytechnic course on Production to further refine my skills in both production and post-production. After entering the Diploma in Media Production and Design in Republic Polytechnic, I further cultivated my interest in editing.
