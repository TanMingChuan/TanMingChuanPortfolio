Financial Literacy Conference 2017

I took part in the Financial Literacy Competition 2016, and was the leader of my team, taking up the role of Director, Editor, and Talent. We were invited to showcase our video and a voting took place to determine the winners. My team came in second after securing the Guest of Honor, Dr Koh Noi Keng's (Director of Centre of Financial Literacy, NIE) vote. We were then invited to showcase our work at the Financial Literacy Conference 2017 at Maybank, an international event.

Personal Projects - Vlogs, events and trips.

I enjoy working on personal projects - whether shot with my GoPro Hero+LCD, or simply my iPhone. I enjoy doing vlogs and fun side-projects as part of events that I attend. Personal Projects - Games and Comedy

One of my favourite things to do is to combine my hobby and my passion - I like to record my own gameplay and edit them into comical sequences that I share with my friends. Although these are just fun side-projects that I attempt when I am free, I feel that they help me refine my editing skills in terms of pacing and audio.
